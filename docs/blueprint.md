# **App Name**: Unverse

## Core Features:

- Wallet Integration & Balance Display: Allows users to connect, disconnect, and reconnect their external crypto wallets. Displays the connected wallet address, real-time ULC balance, 'lockedULC', and 'claimableULC' derived from the ledger and vesting rules. Provides a comprehensive transaction history covering ULC purchases, premium unlocks, subscriptions, tips, claims, and vesting claims, all recorded via the ledger system.
- User Profile & 'My Page' Dashboard: Users can view and manage their personal profiles. The 'My Page' dashboard provides a personalized overview, sourcing ULC balance, 'lockedULC', 'claimableULC', total earnings, and spending history (premium unlocks, tips, subscriptions) directly from the immutable ledger, reflecting all tokenomics and vesting events. Includes a placeholder for owned AI muses.
- Creator Profile & Content Creation: Provides creators with a panel to set up and manage their profile (bio, avatar, cover). Supports uploading public and premium content. Creators can set premium content unlock prices exclusively in ULC and monthly subscription prices exclusively in USDT, adhering strictly to the platform's content economy rules.
- Content Discovery & Interaction: Presents a dynamic 'Discover' feed featuring public content and locked premium content. Users can view content cards, explore creator profiles, unlock premium posts by spending ULC, and subscribe to creators using USDT, aligning with the platform's defined content economy.
- Internal ULC Purchase System: Enables users to purchase ULC internally at a fixed rate of '1 ULC = 0.015 USDT'. This system precisely calculates ULC based on USDT input, credits ULC to the user's internal balance, and routes the corresponding USDT to the 'treasury_wallet' as defined in '/config/system', with all actions recorded in the ledger.
- Token Ledger & Vesting Foundation: Establishes a foundational, ledger-based accounting system as the single source of truth for all economic events. Includes: 'premium_unlock', 'subscription_payment', 'tip', 'ulc_purchase', 'presale_purchase' (12m cliff, 24m linear), 'vesting_claim', 'burn', 'staking_reward', 'creator_payout', 'admin_adjustment', and 'buyback_burn' transactions. Manages all system wallets and implements precise vesting schedules for creators (24 months linear), team tokens (36 months linear, 0-month cliff), and the **Reserve Maturity Protocol (The Seal)** (240 months linear, 24-month cliff). Incorporates tokenomics rules for subscription revenue (85% creator, 15% platform split 67% treasury / 33% buyback burn), premium content commission (15% platform split 67% treasury / 33% burn), and AI services (70% treasury / 30% burn). Includes basic staking foundation elements.
- Admin Panel for System Oversight: Provides highly restricted access to administrators whose connected wallet matches the 'admin_wallet_address' specified in '/config/system'. This comprehensive panel enables oversight of all tokenomics metrics, inspection of balances across all 17 defined system wallets, detailed ledger viewing with filtering capabilities, analysis of presale and vesting statuses, genesis distribution verification, and access to critical '/config/system' parameters for platform management and account moderation (freeze/unfreeze).
- AI Muse Seed Profile Setup (Tool): A specialized tool for defining initial 'seed records' for platform-owned AI Muses (e.g., Isabella, Elena, Chloe) within the '/ai_muses' collection. This feature meticulously structures the data for each muse, including name, category, personality, tone, and flirting level, serving as the essential scaffolding for future advanced generative AI text and image integrations as per the AI system placeholders.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: #7333FF (RGB: 115, 51, 255) – A vibrant, deep purple chosen to evoke a sense of modern digital technology and creativity against a dark background.
- Background Color: #1C191F (RGB: 28, 25, 31) – A very dark, subtly tinted purple for the overall scheme, providing a sophisticated and immersive canvas.
- Accent Color: #95A6FF (RGB: 149, 166, 255) – A lighter, more saturated blue-purple selected to create contrast for interactive elements, highlights, and calls to action, drawing attention while maintaining visual harmony.
- Headline Font: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) – Selected for its contemporary, tech-inspired aesthetic, suitable for impactful headlines and titles across the platform.
- Body Text Font: 'Inter' (sans-serif) – Chosen for its exceptional readability and neutral, objective look, making it ideal for longer blocks of text such as content descriptions, transaction history, and platform information.
- Utilize modern, sleek, and minimalist vector icons with a focus on clarity and quick recognition. Icons should subtly reflect themes of digital currency, social interaction, and creativity, aligning with the platform's futuristic vibe.
- Implement a responsive, mobile-first design with clean, spacious layouts. Employ a card-based structure for content discovery and creator profiles to ensure modularity and ease of navigation across devices.
- Incorporate subtle, performant animations for transitions between pages, interactive elements (e.g., button hovers, content loading), and notifications to enhance user engagement without distracting from core functionality.
